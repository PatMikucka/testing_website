Our Story

Hi, I'm Lila

I started making candles in my kitchen five years ago, frustrated that most candles were full of synthetic fragrances and paraffin wax. I wanted something cleaner — for my home and for the planet. Now every candle I make is 100% vegan, uses only natural waxes and pure essential oils, and comes in packaging you can feel good about.
