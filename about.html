Our Story

Hi, I'm Lila 👋

I started making candles five years ago at my kitchen table in Bristol, mostly out of frustration. Every candle I bought was packed with synthetic fragrance, petroleum-based paraffin wax, and came wrapped in plastic I'd immediately throw away. I wanted something better — for my home, for the air I was breathing, and for the planet.

So I taught myself. I experimented for months with natural waxes, tested hundreds of essential oil combinations, and slowly found the scents that felt right — grounded, real, and beautifully simple. What started as a hobby for friends and family grew into something I couldn't stop.

What I believe in

Every decision I make in this business comes back to these three things.

🌱

100% Vegan

No beeswax, no animal-derived ingredients of any kind. Every candle is fully vegan and cruelty-free — always.

🌍

Sustainable Materials

Natural soy and coconut wax, pure essential oils, cotton and wood wicks, and plastic-free packaging made from recycled materials.

🤲

Small Batch, Made by Hand

Every single candle is poured, labelled, and packed by me. That means I can guarantee the quality and put real care into every order.

How a candle is made

A peek behind the scenes at the process — slow, careful, and done with intention.

1️⃣

Source

Ethically sourced soy and coconut wax, UK-made wicks, and pure essential oils from trusted suppliers.

2️⃣

Blend

Wax is melted slowly to the right temperature, then blended with a carefully measured essential oil combination.

3️⃣

Pour

Each candle is hand-poured into its jar or pot, wick centred, and left to cure for a minimum of 48 hours.

4️⃣

Pack & Send

Every order is hand-packed with recycled tissue and kraft paper, then sent with a handwritten note.
